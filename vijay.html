Vijay

Vijay, popularly known as Thalapathy Vijay, is one of the most successful actors in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, he has acted in many blockbuster films and is admired for his acting, dancing, and action performances. Vijay has a huge fan following across India and around the world. Besides acting, he is known for his charitable activities and has recently entered politics. His dedication, simplicity, and inspiring personality have made him a role model for many people.
